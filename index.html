The No Surprises Act Is Your Ally

Enacted in 2022, the No Surprises Act was designed to protect patients from surprise medical bills. But it was also intended to ensure that out-of-network providers are paid fairly through Independent Dispute Resolution (IDR) arbitration.

Insurance companies often underpay, assuming doctors will simply accept whatever they offer. They're betting on your lack of time and expertise in navigating the appeals process.

  • Legal right to challenge underpayments through arbitration
  • Fair market rate determination by independent arbitrators
  • Statute of limitations‚Äîact now to protect your claims
  • No disruption to your patient care or hospital relationships
